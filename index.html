About Me


Engineer of Arts et Métiers Paristech, I continued my studies by a master’s degree in neurosciences at BME Paris. Graduated since March 2019, I am currently finishing my internship at Campus Biotech Geneva, in the Human Neurosciences platform. Our mission is to support research groups by proposing innovative tools or materials through different common support platforms (data analysis, EEG, MRI, VR, Robotics, Neuromodulation, etc.). My job is to develop easy-to-use EEG signal analysis tools, to make analysis easier for researchers not having the necessary skills in programming and signal processing.

I discovered programming with python during my "classe préparatoire aux grandes écoles". I took a liking to this language and started to explore the countless possibilities it offers, from server creation using Flask or Django, to visualization of data with matplotlib seaborn and bokeh throughmore artistic libraries such as the blender api or pymidi I then began to interest myself in electronics espcially by arduino and raspberries and started creating connected objects. Now I try to use these different tools in various personal or professional projects that I would like to share with you here
